RC Airplanes

My father has built and flown RC airplanes since he was young. As I grew up, we would spend weekend mornings in the hobby-room building models. Above we see me flying an Alpina 5001, with a wing-span of 5 meters.

This picture was taken in the spring of 2009 on the fields a stones-throw from where I grew up. Here with V.S. Carl Golberg's Sr. Falcon which has served as a triner model ever since my father was the same age some 30 yers ago.
